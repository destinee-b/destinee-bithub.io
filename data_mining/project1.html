The Problem

Nike is one of the most popular brands in the world. Utilizing the data from the Nike Global Sales 2024 Dataset, I hope to answer a few questions:

  • ⤷ Which countries contributed the most to Nike's overall revenue?
  • ⤷ What categories of product generate the most revenue?
  • ⤷ Which products are the most popular amongst the best selling countries?
  • ⤷ What percentage of total sales comes from each price segment?

Understanding this data can give insights into trends, business growth, and more.

The Data

The dataset contains information on Nike's global sales for the year 2024 from Kaggle. It includes the following columns:

  • ⤷ Month: The month in which the sale was made (January - December)
  • ⤷ Region: Geographic Area
  • ⤷ Main Category: The main category of the product (Footwear, Apparel, Equipment)
  • ⤷ Product Line: Specific product series or models
  • ⤷ Price Tier: Pricing segment (Premium, Mid-Range, Budget)
  • ⤷ Units Sold: Number of items sold
  • ⤷ Revenue: Total Revenue in US Dollars
  • ⤷ Online Sales Percentage: Percentage of sales through online platforms
  • ⤷ Retail Price: Retail price per unit in USD

What I Learned

One of the first questions I sought out to answer was which regions contributed the most to the total sales of Nike. After analyzing the data, it is clear that Greater China contributed the most, followed by Japan, then America in 3rd. This was not surprising, as I know Nike is still a massive brand in China and Japan also.

Another question I wanted to asnwer was which months led to the highest sales. I was not surprised that there was a large spike at the end of the year because of the holidays.

I finally answered the question of which products had the highest sales. I am not even sure what the top seller is, so when I looked it up to be a sports shoe, I was shocked. I expected the Nike Dunk or similar to be the highest selling.
